Chewy Brownie

What are chewy brownies

Chewy brownies are a type of brownie that have a dense, moist, and slightly gooey texture. They are characterized by their rich chocolate flavor and chewy consistency, which is achieved by using ingredients like butter, sugar, eggs, cocoa powder, and flour. Chewy brownies are a popular treat and are often enjoyed on their own or with a scoop of ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup baking cocoa
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons light corn syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (Optional)
  • ¼ cup confectioners' sugar
  • 4 teaspoons water
